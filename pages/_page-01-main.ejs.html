The first look at finances after a surprising primary race for governor shows that DFL'er Tim Walz has still outraised Republican Jeff Johnson by a healthy margin, but then again, Johnson had been far behind Tim Pawlenty in fundraising before the primary, too.

Walz has spent nearly $3 million so far, while Johnson has yet to crack the $1 million mark for spending. With a mere six weeks to Election Day, both candidates have hefty war chests in excess of $1 million that they can spend. Expect to see ads ramp up from both the candidates themselves and political action committees on both sides of the aisle.

Figures below include filings starting Jan. 1, 2017 and ending Sept. 18, 2018. For context, we've retained former candidates in the list who competed in the August primary, but who have not been required to file a September report. Their figures are through the end of July.
